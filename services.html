Meet Dr. Janie Forbes

Dr. Janie Forbes is a board-certified physician who combines the best of modern medicine with time-tested healing traditions. With extensive training in both conventional medicine and integrative therapies, she brings a unique, comprehensive approach to healthcare that addresses the root cause of illness while providing immediate symptom relief.

Her expertise spans medical acupuncture, integrative medicine, and primary care, allowing her to create personalized treatment plans that draw from multiple healing traditions. Dr. Forbes is committed to spending quality time with each patient, listening to their concerns, and developing lasting partnerships for optimal health outcomes.

Medical Acupuncture

Medical acupuncture is the integration of evidence-based, modern western medicine and traditional acupuncture. Dr. Forbes utilizes several acupuncture techniques in her practice, including Kiko Matsumoto Style (KMS), auricular acupuncture, Chinese scalp acupuncture, and trigger point dry needling. The visit includes review of your medical history, a physical exam when indicated, and personalized lifestyle, herbal, and pharmaceutical recommendations to improve symptoms and address the underlying root cause of disease.
