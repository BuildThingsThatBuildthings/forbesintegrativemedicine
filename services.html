Medical Acupuncture

Medical acupuncture is the integration of evidence-based, modern western medicine and traditional acupuncture. Dr. Forbes utilizes several acupuncture techniques in her practice, including Kiko Matsumoto Style (KMS), auricular acupuncture, Chinese scalp acupuncture, and trigger point dry needling.

Benefits

  • Immediate relief from pain, migraines, and digestive issues
  • Treatment for chronic pain and hormone imbalances
  • Anxiety and stress management
