Meet Dr. Janie Forbes

Dr. Janie Forbes is a board-certified Family Medicine physician and is trained in (integrated structural) medical acupuncture. She graduated from the Penn State College of Medicine and completed her residency in Family Medicine at the University of Virginia, where she also served as chief resident. Following residency, Dr. Forbes received her medical acupuncture training at the University of New England College of Osteopathic Medicine.

Her dedication to integrative and holistic healing extends to Ayurvedic medicine, having studied under the renowned Dr. Vasant Lad at the Ayurvedic Institute, where she earned a certification in Marma Therapy. Before relocating to sunny Phoenix, AZ with her family, Dr. Forbes served as an Assistant Professor at the University of Virginia School of Medicine. At present, she is a part-time faculty member at the University of Arizona College of Medicine. Dr. Forbes is licensed in both Arizona and Virginia.

What is Medical Acupuncture?

Medical acupuncture is the integration of evidence-based, modern western medicine and traditional acupuncture. Dr. Forbes utilizes several acupuncture techniques in her practice, including Kiko Matsumoto Style (KMS), auricular acupuncture, Chinese scalp acupuncture, and trigger point dry needling. The visit includes review of your medical history, a physical exam when indicated, and personalized lifestyle, herbal, and pharmaceutical recommendations to improve symptoms and address the underlying root cause of disease.

Principle 1: Integration of Traditional and Modern Practices

East Meets West: We combine time-tested acupuncture techniques with the latest medical research, ensuring a comprehensive approach to your health and well-being.

Principle 2: Focus on Overall Wellness

Holistic Health: Our treatments address not just symptoms, but the root causes of health issues, promoting balance and wellness throughout your entire body and mind.

Principle 3: Personalized Treatment Plans

Tailored for You: We create individualized treatment plans, recognizing that each patient's health journey is unique and requires a personalized approach. Our goal is for patients to truly feel cared for and enjoy a concierge-like experience.
